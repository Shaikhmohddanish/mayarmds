Explore our capabilities

We offer services in different engineering sectors.

Pipe Stress analysis

Static Stress Analysis complying international codes and standards viz. ASME B31.1, B31.3, B31.4, B31.8, AS-2885, ISO14692. Analysis of critical systems like Column re-boiler, Air Fin Coolers, Pumps, Turbine, Compressors, Heaters, Jacketed Piping etc.

Piping Design

We possess expertise development of Piping G.A., Isometrics, MTO, 2D Drafting. Prepearing Piping Material Specifications, Data Sheets etc.

Pipeline Engineering

We provide services such as Preparation of Pipeline alignment sheets, station approach drawings, specifications, Stress analysis, surge analysis, pipeline calculations etc.

FEA & CFD

Solution to critical problems such as local stress evaluation of nozzles, special support design, buckling anaysis for ducts, SIF calculations, Vibrations, remaining life assessment, flow simulation, flow induced vibrations etc.

Surge / Water Hammer Analysis

We provide complete engineering solution for the surge / water hammer analysis including Flow calculations and Dynamic stress analysis. Surge analysis for Sudden Valve closure, Pump trip cases.

Pressure Vessel Design

Design / Mechanical data sheets for Storage Tanks, Exchangers, Vessels etc. Checking of engineering documents, calculations and drawings on behalf of Owner, Operating company. Detailed fabrication drawings with welding details, code compliance.
