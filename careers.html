Career at mayarmds

At mayarmds , we provide two types of employments.

One for our in –house need where we bring out the best in our people by providing them with fantastic opportunities promoting their learning, motivation and passion to excel. We look forward to dedicated engineers who aspire to be the clock builders and not just time tellers. Here we encourage engineers who look career not only as a monetary gain but also considers learnings, self-branding, research, recognition and health as important aspects of career.

In other type, we depute candidates on third party contract to renowned multi national companies on 6 months to one year contract in multiple disciplines such as – PIPING, PIPELINES, PDMS, PDS,SP3D, Engineers and designers, Electrical, instrumentation, civil, process engineers and designers.
