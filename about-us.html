About Mayarmds Pvt. Ltd.

From Oil & Gas Piping to Chemical & Refineries, Pharmaceutical, Power Piping and Pipeline Industries. We possess knowledge, competencies and experience to solve your critical engineering problems and make your everyday operations more efficient.

We ‘value’ our clients by providing Ethical, Economical, Effective and Efficient engineering solutions. We believe in continuous improvement and aspire to build long term relation with our clients.

Mayarmds PVT.LTD. is a specialized engineering consultancy providing engineering and design solutions in the field of Process & Petrochemical Industry. We value our clients by providing Ethical, Economical, Effective and Efficient engineering solutions. Our focused, pragmatic and resourceful team ensures that we enable our clients to achieve their targets and aspirations.

Mayarmds PVT.LTD. is a fast growing and rising company in the dynamic field of Piping Technology. We specialize in Design and Engineering (Static and Dynamic Stress analysis) of Piping and Pipeline Projects. We provide Water Hammer / Surge analysis solutions along with Dynamic pipe stress analysis under one roof. We also have expertise in Finite Element Analysis (FEA), Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) solving specifically Process Plant problems.

We are a team of experienced Piping, Process and Civil Engineers. In addition, we also have a bank of expert engineers with Ph.Ds. to tap into for any advanced and critical subject matter expertise. We ensure that resources are optimally utilized at every stage of our process by providing client with best value for their investment.
