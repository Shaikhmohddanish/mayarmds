Trainings

PROTTON Engineering conducts Piping and Pipeline Stress Analysis training for Fresh Graduates and experienced engineers who are keen on learning Pipe / Pipeline Stress Analysis using CAESAR II software. Our training sessions cover software as well as concepts of Stress Analysis and therefore help our attendees to develop engineering judgment, code interpretation and much more.

Our Lead Trainer, Mr. Gaurav Bhende, has conducted several Professional Training Courses on Stress Analysis using CAESAR II software in numerous international engineering companies and Institutes as a freelance Trainer since 2009.

Mr. Gaurav was also invited to participated as a speaker on Effect of Modelling structure using CAESAR II’s Structure Module on Static and Seismic Analysis at Hexagon, Las Vegas in 2013 He has been invited in CAU Express- The Tour of India 2015 to 2018, as a guest speaker on topic such as Buried pipe analysis, flange leakage, WRC 107 / WRC 297 nozzle evaluation etc.
